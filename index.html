Beheeyem

The fast, reliable and open-source Discord bot designed to suit your Pokémon information-related needs.

Get all the information you need for 

Beheeyem provides you with all the Pokémon-related information you need. He can provide you useful info such as:

  • Pokédex entries
  • Type-damage calculation
  • Move damage and effects
  • Ability descriptions
  • Unit conversion
  • Item effects

and a lot more. Enter b.help in a message to get a list of common commands you can use with Beheeyem.
